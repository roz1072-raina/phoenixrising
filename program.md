# 🌱 Coaching Program

## ✨ Program Name
**Reparent. Regulate. Rewrite. Rise.™**

---

## 🧭 Program Overview

This program helps clients:
- Reconnect with themselves  
- Regulate emotions  
- Rewrite internal narratives  
- Rise into a new identity

### 🌊 Module 2: Regulate (Stability)
**Goal:** Build emotional control and safety  
**Key Focus:**
- Nervous system regulation  
- Emotional awareness  
- Grounding in the present  
**Exercises:**
- Deep breathing (inhale 4, hold 4, exhale 6)  
- 5-4-3-2-1 grounding technique  
- Body awareness check-ins  
#### 💬 Question Bank
- What are you feeling right now?
  - Where do you feel it in your body?
  - Is it heavy, tight, or light?
- Can you name the emotion you're experiencing?
  - If you had to give it a word, what would it be?
  - Does it shift as you sit with it?
- What helps you feel safe in this moment?
  - What has helped you before?
  - What do you need right now?
- What happens when you slow down your breathing?
  - What do you notice changing?
  #### 🪜 Sessions

**Session 1: Emotional Awareness & Regulation**
- Focus: Helping client identify and regulate emotions  
- Activity: Breathing + grounding exercise  

---

**Session Flow:**

1. **Check-in**
   - “What are you feeling right now?”

2. **Body Awareness**
   - Where do you feel it in your body?

3. **Regulation Practice**
   - Guide breathing (inhale 4, hold 4, exhale 6)

4. **Grounding**
   - 5-4-3-2-1 exercise

5. **Reflection**
   - What changed after slowing down?

6. **Close**
   - What can you use from this during the week?

### 🔥 Module 3: Rewrite (Transformation)

**Goal:** Shift beliefs and thought patterns  
**Goal:** Shift beliefs and thought patterns  

**Key Focus:**
- Limiting beliefs  
- Internal narratives  
- Identity awareness  

**Exercises:**
- Thought reframing  
- Affirmation building  
- Belief journaling  
#### 💬 Question Bank

- What belief is driving this thought?
  - What are you telling yourself right now?
  - When did you first learn this?

- Is this thought true—or familiar?
  - Have you always believed this?
  - Where did it come from?

- What would a more supportive thought look like?
  - What would you say to someone you love?
  - What feels more aligned with who you want to be?

- Who would you be without this belief?
  - How would you think differently?
  - How would you show up?
    #### 🪜 Sessions

**Session 1: Identifying & Rewriting Beliefs**
- Focus: Recognizing and shifting limiting beliefs  
- Activity: Thought reframing  

---

**Session Flow:**

1. **Check-in**
   - “What thoughts have been coming up for you?”

2. **Identify Belief**
   - What belief is behind that thought?
   - Is it true—or familiar?

3. **Challenge the Belief**
   - Where did this come from?
   - Is it still serving you?

4. **Rewrite**
   - What would a more supportive belief look like?

5. **Identity Shift**
   - Who would you be without this belief?

6. **Close**
   - What new thought will you practice this week?

### 🌟 Module 4: Rise (Integration)

**Goal:** Step into a new identity  
**Goal:** Step into a new identity and apply growth in real life  

**Key Focus:**
- Identity embodiment  
- Consistent action  
- Confidence building  

**Exercises:**
- Weekly goal setting  
- Habit tracking  
- Self-reflection check-ins  
#### 💬 Question Bank

- What version of yourself are you stepping into?
  - How does she think?
  - How does she respond?

- What does that version of you do differently?
  - What habits does she have?
  - What does she no longer tolerate?

- What is one action you can take this week?
  - What’s small but meaningful?
  - What feels aligned?

- What is holding you back from fully showing up?
  - Is it fear, doubt, or habit?
  - What would happen if you moved anyway?
  #### 🪜 Sessions

**Session 1: Embodying the New Identity**
- Focus: Taking aligned action and reinforcing growth  
- Activity: Identity + action planning  

---

**Session Flow:**

1. **Check-in**
   - “What has shifted for you recently?”

2. **Identity Activation**
   - Who are you becoming?
   - What feels different about you?

3. **Behavior Alignment**
   - What does this version of you do daily?
   - What needs to change in your routine?

4. **Action Planning**
   - What is one action you will take this week?
   - When will you do it?

5. **Remove Blocks**
   - What might stop you?
   - How will you respond differently?

6. **Close**
   - What are you committing to this week?
